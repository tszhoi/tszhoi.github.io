Redefining video editing with Jupitrr AI

Launched in Apr 2023, Bootstrapped.

In 2019, I was doing my master’s in London, and Covid brought me back to Hong Kong. I reconnected with Harris and Jerome (my former bosses at Freehunter), and suddenly, we started working on a new project called Jupitrr. Our goal was to help freelancers and small businesses kickstart their ventures.

Since then, we’ve gone through a few pivots (6 Months Learning - Building an Audiogram Tool & From the first $6.99 in 2 years to $12K in a week).

In 2023, we landed on the idea of an AI-powered video editor—a fast and easy way for small business owners to create content. We specialised in B-roll generation, includes Stock Videos, GIFs, Web Images, Charts, Graphs and many more.

As a Product Co-founder, my role focuses on talking to users, designing the experience, organising the roadmap, and frontend coding. 2024 has been a year of growth for us, winning Product of the Week at Product Hunt, expanding from 5K users to over 100K+ users, with our MRR growing 12% week-on-week.

There’s so much to document about Jupitrr, but for now, let's focusing on building.
